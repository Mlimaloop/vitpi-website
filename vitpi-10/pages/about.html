About

Graham Noble

Wealth Manager · Entrepreneur · Founder

THE STORY

28 years of doing this properly.

I started in financial services nearly three decades ago with a simple belief: that independent, unconflicted advice — delivered by someone who genuinely understands your life — produces better outcomes than anything a large institution can offer.

Vitpi grew from that belief — becoming the home for everything I do in wealth management, passive income architecture, portfolio strategy and pension planning.

I'm based in Dubai, working with clients across the Middle East, UK and internationally. Most are HNW individuals, expats and entrepreneurs whose financial lives don't fit a standard template.

"The wealth I've built for clients has always been the means to an end — the freedom to live the life they actually want."

Career timeline

A career built on trust.

1997
Entered financial services
Began a career in wealth management and financial planning, working with private clients across the UK and internationally. Built a reputation on independent, client-first advice.
Early 2000s
Expanded into international markets
Developed a specialism in cross-border wealth management and expat financial planning — particularly for UK nationals relocating to the Middle East and beyond.
2010s
Established Dubai base · Founded Vitpi
Relocated to Dubai and formalised the practice as Vitpi — a boutique firm built entirely around client outcomes, with no product sales targets and no institutional conflicts.
2022
Co-founded Vertical Sky
Launched an ethical Kilimanjaro climbing company with head guide Zidane Juma — bringing the same values of integrity and craft to the adventure travel space.
2023
Founded MlimaLoop
Launched East Africa's first integrated rPET recycling business in Arusha, Tanzania — deploying 28 years of financial discipline into building a circular economy venture with real environmental impact.
Today
Vitpi · Dubai · Serving clients globally
Continuing to grow Vitpi's wealth management practice while building two new ventures in East Africa. The financial expertise and the entrepreneurial conviction have always been the same thing.

Beyond Vitpi
